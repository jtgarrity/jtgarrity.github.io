Jon Garrity

Boston, MA | jon@tagup.io | 617-804-1401

I live in Boston and am the CEO of Tagup Inc., building a web-based platform to predict industrial equipment failures. Previously, I was a software product manager at GE, where I helped developed the Grid IQ Insight platform. My professional interests revolve around machine learning in industrial settings, energy systems, and the Internet of Things. Outside of work, my interests include physics, music, and economics. Before jumping into the tech world, I earned an S.B. in Physics and in Economics from MIT, and an M.B.A. from Harvard Business School.
