Welcome to the Industrial Internet

Tagup is the first end-to-end Industrial Internet solution for the power and electric utility industry.

Simple & Efficient Deployment

Connect your products to the web in an under an hour.

1. Attach our Industrial Internet Gateway

Easy to connect via serial (RS-232/RS-485) or via Ethernet. Data from your sensor, PLC, or RTU is parsed and sent securely to our web servers. Data can be sent via cellular, WiFi, or a hardwired connection if required. Our gateway fits in the palm of your hand, with an optional NEMA/IEC 60529 compliant version for harsh environments.

2. Data stored and analyzed

Our platform can handle anything, from sub-second readings on hundreds of sensors on up. Data can be stored, searched, and visualized across an entire fleet. Our team's expertise is machine learning and artificial intelligence - honed at MIT's Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. We build models to predict asset failures before they occur.

3. Access data and alarm notifications anytime

View important alerts at a unit and fleet level via our easy to use web application. Real-time asset data can be accessed using your mobile phone or computer, from anywhere in the world, at any time of the day. Say goodbye to the industrial user interfaces from the 90's, and embrace a modern user experience with Tagup.

Tested Remote Access & Security Technologies

Provide 24/7 expert support to your end customers. Identify equipment failures before they occur.

Connect products to the web in minutes

With our patent-pending platform, we can connect industrial products to the Internet in less than an hour. Once connected, you can view operational (like sensor data) and non-operational data (for example, site photos) remotely. Among other protocols, we can integrate products using:

  1. MODBUS RTU/ASCII
  2. DNP3
  3. IEC 61850

Military-Grade Security

We've built trusted security technologies right into the core of the Tagup platform. All data is encrypted, assets are checked for authorized connections, and our embedded industrial web servers include a DMZ to ensure no external control of your products.

  1. SSL/256 bit AES encrypted transport
  2. NERC-CIP compliant
  3. Robust permissions management

Access data and analytics anywhere

Our application can be accessed on any mobile device. Want to see how your circuit breakers, transformers, or generators are performing via your smart phone? No problem. Additionally, our advanced analytics suite, developed at MIT, can help identify asset failures before they occur. Move your organization from reactive to proactive.
