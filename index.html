Jon Garrity

Cambridge, MA | jon@tagup.io | 617-804-1401

Bio

Jon is founder and CEO of Tagup, building a web-based platform to reduce industrial equipment failures. Previously, he was software product manager at GE, where he helped developed the Grid IQ Insight platform. His interests revolve around Internet-enabled remote monitoring and machine learning applications in industrial settings. Jon earned his S.B. in Physics and in Economics from MIT, and his M.B.A. from Harvard Business School.
